## Maximal Paralleles System

`d=2∗2` `x=2∗25`   `b=8∗5`  `z=2∗5`

`g=d∗3`  `a=x∗2` `y=b∗3` `h=d∗5`  

`i=g∗h`  `u=y∗z`  

`v=a∗i`

`c=v/u`

| **P1.4** | `z = 2 * 5`
